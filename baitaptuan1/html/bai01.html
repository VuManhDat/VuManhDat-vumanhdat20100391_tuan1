One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begin re-evoluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs whithin his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquaited with the inner city streets, and relizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courst. The Street Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and interesting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client.
